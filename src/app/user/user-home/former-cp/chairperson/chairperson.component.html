Hon’ble Justice Indira Jain
Profile
Tenure: 26/12/2018 - 19/12/2022

Hon’ble Former Chairperson, Mahareat (Retd.) Maharashtra Real Estate Appellate Tribunal for States of Maharashtra and Goa w.e.f. ).

Educational Qualifications:

B.A, LLB, LLM (National Merit and ULG Scholarship Holder) Stood first in the order of merit in BGL & LLB Examinations in 1978.
Achievements • Recipient of Motilal Nehru Centenary Gold Medal .
• Narendraji Tidke Gold Medalist .
• Bhaiya Saheb Mankeshwar Gold Medalist.
• Rai Sahib R.Banerji Silver Medalist.
• Diwan Bahadur R. Pandit Silver Medalist.
• Shri Chimanlal Setalwad Golden Jubilee Prize winner.
• Shri Chondro Ghosh Memorial Prize winner.

Prominent Positions held in Govt. of Maharashtra/ India:
1. Worked as Auditor in the office of the Accountant General Maharashtra II, Nagpur.
2. Appointed as Lecturer in Post Graduate Teaching Department in Law, Nagpur University.
3. Joined Judicial Service in the cadre of Civil Judge , Junior Division and Judicial Magistrate, First Class (1984).
4. Joint Director and Director of JOTI (Maharashtra) Nagpu and Joint Director of Maharashtra Judicial Academy and Indian Mediation Centre and Training Institute, Uttan.
5. Worked as common District and Sessions Judge, Union Territory of Dadra and Nagar Haveli, Daman and Diu.
6. Principal District and Sessions Judge, Yavatmal, Jalgaon and Nasik and Administrative Member Industrial Court at Nagpur.
7. Registrar, High Court of Judicature at Bombay, Bench at Goa and Principal, Government Law College, Mumbai. Elevated as Additional Judge of the Bombay High Court on 1st January, 2015.
8. Appointed as Chairperson , MahaREAT
