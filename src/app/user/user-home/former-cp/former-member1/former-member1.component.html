Hon’ble S.S. Sandhu
Profile
Tenure: 26/12/2018 - 07/07/2023

Hon’ble Former Member, (Tech/Administrative), Maharashtra Real Estate Appellate Tribunal for States of Maharashtra and Goa w.e.f. 24th December, 2018 to 08th July, 2023).

Educational Qualifications:

M.A. (Political Science), M.A. in Development Administration (UK), and Bachelor of Laws (LLB).
Joined Indian Police Service (1983-Punjab Cadre)
Joined Indian Administrative Service (1984-Maharashtra Cadre)

Prominent Positions held in Govt. of Maharashtra/ India:
1. Chief Executive Officer Solapur and Parbhani Zilla Parishads
2. Collector and District Magistrate Parbhani and Jalgaon Districts
3. Chief Officer, Mumbai Housing Board (MHADA)
4. Zonal Joint Director General, Foreign Trade (Western Zone- Maharashtra, Gujarat, Goa, Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh States)
5. Divisional Commissioner, Konkan (Mumbai) Division
6. Principal Secretary Rural Development Department Government of Maharashtra
7. Principal Secretary Revenue and Forest Department, Government of Maharashtra
8. Addl Chief Secretary (Co-Operation), Department of Co-operation, Marketing and Textile, Government of Maharashtra till retirement on 31st July 2018.
