Introduction
  • Maharashtra Real Estate Appellate Tribunal, Fort Mumbai office started on 24.12.2018. The Government of Maharashtra has established the Maharashtra Real Estate Appellate Tribunal Office to appeal against the decision, direction or order given by the Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority (Maharera) if the complainant is not satisfied with that decision, direction or order.
Objectives of Real Estate (Regulation & Development) Act, 2016
  • To file an appeal against the decision, direction or order given by the office of Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority (MAHRERA) in respect of registered or unregistered construction projects if the complainant person or organization is not satisfied with that decision, direction or order.
  • To verify the appeals filed in the Maharashtra Real Estate Appellate Tribunal and get due justice to the complainant person or organization at the earliest.
  • Providing certified copy of judgments, directions or orders passed through the office of Maharashtra Real Estate Appellate Tribunal.
  • To help the person or organization who comes to the Maharashtra Real Estate Appellate Tribunal office for justice by providing proper guidance to get justice.
Jurisdiction
  • The Maharashtra Real Estate Appellate Tribunal Office includes Maharashtra State, the Union Territories of Diu Daman and Dadra Nagar Haveli and Also, from the year 2021, appeals related to the Real Estate Regulatory Authority of Goa State come under this office.
